GSteg

Steganography thing for university thing. Things are involved at places. Yay ^-^

Encode

Very simple, depends on "in.txt" containing the text, "in.bmp" image and "out.bmp" image. Does what you expect, moves bits, magic happens, yadda yadda.

Decode

Also the same, depends on "out.bmp", outputs the text. Bits are moved, yadda yadda.

Gtkmm Interface

Very, very WIP, currently just an image viewer. Next step is get the image label to render in a fixed frame and I can be at peace in life.
